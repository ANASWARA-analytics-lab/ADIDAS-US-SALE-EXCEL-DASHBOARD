# ABOUT THE DATASET (CLEANED)

- Invoice_Date: Date on which the order was placed (used for month/day trends and time filters)
- Year: Calendar year extracted from Order_Date for year-level filtering.
- Month: Month name or number extracted from Order_Date for monthly analysis and slicers.
- Region: Sales region within the US (e.g., North, South, East, West) used in the “Sales by Region” chart.
- State/City (optional): More detailed location information if available for deeper geographic analysis.
- Retailer: Name of the retailer or sales partner (used in “Sales and Profit by Retailers”).
- Retailer_ID : Used to uniqely identify the retailers
- Product: High-level grouping such as Footwear, Apparel, Accessories (used in operations/efficiency view).
- Sales_Method: Channel through which the order was made (In‑store, Online, Outlet).
- Units_Sold: Quantity of items sold in the transaction, aggregated for unit-based charts.
- Price_per_unit: Selling price per unit of the product.
- Total_Sales: Revenue for the transaction (Units_Sold × Unit_Price), used in all sales charts and KPIs.
- Profit: Difference between Total_Sales and Total_Cost, used in profit KPIs and charts.
- Operating_margin : An important profitability ratio measuring revenue after the deduction of operating expenses.
